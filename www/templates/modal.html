How to Login

 Every student has an @ocsbstudent.ca email address.
Yours is your firstname.lastname@ocsbstudent.ca and your password is the same as the one you use to log onto school computers.
By default, your password is Welcome2FL (Replace F with your first initial and L with your last initial).
If you still have trouble logging in, ask a librarian for help setting up your account.

For Parents:
Have your child login with their account. You will remain logged in until you log out.
